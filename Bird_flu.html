Bird Flu Monitoring

New bird flu outbreaks in poultry farms are being closely monitored to prevent wider spread.

About Bird Flu Monitoring

Bird flu is a virus that mainly spreads among birds like chickens and ducks. Sometimes it can infect humans and other animals. Health workers are closely watching bird flu cases to stop the disease from spreading quickly.

Why Are Cases Rising

Experts say bird flu cases are increasing because wild birds can carry the virus to farms and markets. Cold weather, animal movement, and crowded poultry areas also make the virus spread more easily.

How to Stay Safe

  • Avoid touching sick or dead birds
  • Wash your hands after being near animals/li>
  • Keep poultry areas clean
  • Wear a mask and gloves when handling birds
  • Report unusual bird deaths to local authorities
