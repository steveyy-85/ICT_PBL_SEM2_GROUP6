ME DNA Research

Researchers are starting a huge DNA study on ME/chronic fatigue syndrome to better understand the illness and maybe find treatments in the future.

About ME DNA Research

ME, also called Chronic Fatigue Syndrome, is a long-term illness that causes extreme tiredness, body pain, sleep problems, and difficulty concentrating. Scientists are now starting large DNA studies to learn more about the disease and find possible treatments.

Why Is Research Increasing

Health experts say more research is happening because many people around the world are affected by ME. After the COVID-19 pandemic, doctors also noticed that some patients developed similar long-term symptoms, making scientists more interested in studying the illness.

How to Stay Healthy

  • Get enough rest and sleep
  • Eat healthy and balanced meals
  • Drink plenty of water
  • Avoid too much stress
  • Exercise gently if recommended by a doctor
  • Visit a doctor if tiredness lasts for a long time
