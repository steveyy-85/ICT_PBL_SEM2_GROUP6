Dengue Cases Still Rising

Stay aware and protect yourself from dengue fever.

About Dengue

Dengue is a dangerous illness caused by mosquito bites. Many countries are seeing more dengue cases this year because of hot weather and heavy rain. Doctors are warning people to stay careful and remove standing water near their homes.

Why Are Cases Rising

Health experts say dengue cases are increasing because mosquitoes can grow faster in warm temperatures. Flooding and dirty water also make it easier for mosquitoes to spread.

How to Stay Safe

  • Use mosquito spray or cream
  • Wear long sleeve clothes
  • Do not leave water open outside
  • Keep windows and doors closed
