Smoking and Vaping

Some countries are making stricter anti-smoking and vaping laws in 2026 to reduce health problems caused by tobacco and vapes.

About Smoking and Vaping

Smoking and vaping can harm the lungs and body. Cigarettes contain harmful chemicals, while vapes also have nicotine and other substances that may affect health. Doctors say both can cause breathing problems and addiction.

Why Are Cases Rising

Health experts say more teenagers and young adults are smoking and vaping because of stress, social media trends, and flavored vape products. Some people also think vaping is safer than smoking, which increases its use.

How to Stay Safe

  • Avoid smoking and vaping products
  • Do not try cigarettes or vapes because of peer pressure
  • Spend time with friends who support healthy habits
  • Exercise and drink plenty of water
  • Learn about the health risks of nicotine
  • Ask for help if someone wants to quit smoking or vaping
