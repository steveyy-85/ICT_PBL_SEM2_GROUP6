    Stay informed with the latest health tips, medical updates, and important news to help you make better decisions for your well-being.

    Dengue Cases Still Rising

    Health experts warn that dengue fever is still spreading in tropical regions, so mosquito control is very important.

    Hantavirus

    Is a rare virus spread by rats and mice. It can cause fever and serious breathing problems.

    Bird Flu Monitoring

    New bird flu outbreaks in poultry farms are being closely monitored to prevent wider spread.

    Smoking and Vaping

    Some countries are making stricter anti-smoking and vaping laws in 2026 to reduce health problems caused by tobacco and vapes.

    Measles Cases Rise Again

    Measles cases are rising again in some countries, especially in Bangladesh and parts of the US. Health officials say lower vaccination rates after the pandemic may be one reason.

    ME DNA Research

    Researchers are starting a huge DNA study on ME/chronic fatigue syndrome to better understand the illness and maybe find treatments in the future.
