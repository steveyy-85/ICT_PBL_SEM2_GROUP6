    Medicine Guide

    Paracetamol:

    • Used for: Fever, Headache, Mild pain
    • How to use: Take only the recommended dose on the package.
    • Important: Taking too much can seriously harm the liver.

    Ibuprofen:

    • Used for: Pain, swelling, fever
    • How to use: Usually taken with food or water to reduce stomach irritation.
    • Important: Avoid overusing it because it may affect the stomach or kidneys.

    Antihistamine:

    • Used for: Allergies, itching, sneezing
    • How to use: Follow age and dosage instructions carefully.
    • Important: Some antihistamines can make you sleepy.

    Oral Rehydration Salts:

    • Used for: Dehydration from diarrhea, vomiting, or heavy sweating
    • How to use: Mix with clean water exactly as instructed on the packet.
    • Important: Helps replace lost fluids and minerals.

    Antacid:

    • Used for: Heartburn, indigestion, stomach acid
    • How to use: Use for short-term relief only.
    • Important: Don’t take large amounts for many days unless a doctor says so.
