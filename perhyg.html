    Why it's important to do Personal Hygiene:

    Personal hygiene is very important because it helps us stay healthy and clean every day. When we take care of our body, we can prevent many illnesses and infections from spreading. Brushing our teeth daily keeps our mouth fresh and protects our teeth from cavities. Taking regular showers removes dirt, sweat, and bacteria from our skin. Washing our hands before eating and after using the toilet helps stop germs from entering our body. Clean clothes also make us feel comfortable and confident around other people. Personal hygiene can improve our appearance and make a good impression on others. It also helps us avoid bad smells and skin problems. Students who practice good hygiene can focus better at school because they feel fresh and comfortable. Good hygiene habits also show responsibility and self-respect. Keeping our nails clean and trimmed prevents dirt and bacteria from building up. Hair care is also important because clean hair keeps the scalp healthy. Hygiene is not only about physical health but also about mental well-being. Feeling clean can improve our mood and confidence. Parents and teachers often encourage hygiene because it creates a safer and healthier environment for everyone. If people ignore personal hygiene, diseases can spread more easily in homes, schools, and public places. Therefore, practicing personal hygiene every day is an important habit that helps us live a healthy, confident, and happy life.
